About

If your looking for a down-to-earth team player who brings an enthusiastic drive to problem solving and loves experimenting with new technologies. Let's connect! Starting as a freshman at the University of Minnesota, my interests in computer science sprung from working at a local restaurant, Punch Neapolitan Pizza. After working there throughout high school I got very familiar with the business, becoming one of their top level cooks. While working many hours trying to fund my college bill, I continuously got questions from customers on Punch’s new online ordering system, and why they didn’t have a mobile app. I built a UX prototype in Sketch and later presented it to the company, at the same time, realizing there is much more that goes into building web apps. My journey at Prime Digital Academy started, soon after, where I entered a fast paced learning environment, specific to full stack web development. During that time Punch's mobile app was released. Check it out!. Since my passion for software development began, I have worked a number of individual and group projects applying the tools and technologies I have learned. Check out my work below!
